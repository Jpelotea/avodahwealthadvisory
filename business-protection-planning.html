Business & legacy planning

Business protection planning for continuity, key people, and employee benefits.

Avodah supports business owners and organizations reviewing business continuation, key person insurance, employee benefits, group HMO, corporate insurance, and financial wellness seminars.

Ask About Business ProtectionView Services

Who this is for

  • Business owners who want to protect operations from key person, cash flow, or continuity risks.
  • Companies reviewing employee benefits, group life, group HMO, or executive protection.
  • Partners discussing buy and sell agreement funding or succession concerns.
  • Teams that want a company financial wellness seminar.

How Avodah helps

Avodah helps clarify the business concern, people involved, possible protection gaps, and practical information to prepare before discussing provider options.

  • Identify key people, employee groups, and operational concerns.
  • Discuss business continuation, key employee protection, and corporate insurance needs.
  • Prepare basic company, employee count, and preferred benefit details.
  • Keep expectations realistic and provider-dependent.

Details to prepare

  • Business type, location, years operating, and number of employees.
  • Main concern: continuity, key person risk, employee benefits, group HMO, or corporate protection.
  • Decision makers and preferred consultation schedule.
  • Existing benefits, policies, or provider arrangements if available.

Final product availability, eligibility, pricing, approval, and coverage decisions remain subject to provider requirements.
