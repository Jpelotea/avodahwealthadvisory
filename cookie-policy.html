Cookie Notice

Last updated: July 24, 2026

This notice explains how Avodah Wealth Advisory uses cookies and similar browser technologies on this website. It should be read together with the Privacy Policy.

Essential website storage

The website may use session storage or similar technologies to preserve navigation state, consultation attribution, a generated submission reference, and the connection between an intake submission and an optional booking step. These technologies support requested website functions and are not intended to create advertising profiles.

Analytics

The website currently uses Google Analytics 4 to understand general website activity and non-personal conversion events. Analytics must not receive names, email addresses, mobile numbers, free-text form answers, or other information that directly identifies a visitor.

Campaign attribution

When a visitor arrives through an advertisement, social post, email, or shared campaign link, the website may record limited source information such as UTM parameters, a campaign identifier, the landing-page path, and a sanitized referring page. This helps Avodah understand which public campaigns lead to consultation requests.

Advertising technologies

Approval-dependent feature: Meta Pixel or other non-essential advertising technologies should be activated only after Avodah approves the tracking purpose, consent behavior, data-sharing settings, and corresponding privacy wording. This page does not by itself activate those technologies.

Your controls

Browser settings can block or delete cookies and website storage. Blocking essential storage may affect attribution, form confirmation, or optional booking continuity. A website preference control for non-essential technologies will be added when Avodah’s final consent configuration is approved.

Changes to this notice

This notice should be reviewed whenever analytics, advertising, embedded content, or consent tools change. Material updates will be reflected by changing the date above.

Contact

Questions about this notice or website privacy may be sent to avodahwealthadvisory@gmail.com.
