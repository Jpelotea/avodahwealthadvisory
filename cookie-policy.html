Cookie Notice and Preferences

Last updated: July 24, 2026

Essential storage

Essential session or local storage supports requested form, booking, attribution, security, confirmation, and preference functions. It remains available when optional tracking is declined.

Analytics

Optional Google Analytics 4 measurement is used only after analytics consent. Events are restricted to non-personal workflow, page, campaign, status, and interaction information. Names, email addresses, mobile numbers, form messages, policy references, resumes, and appointment notes are prohibited from analytics payloads.

Marketing technologies

Approval-dependent: Meta Pixel and similar marketing tracking remain disabled. The preference control displays the category but does not activate Meta Pixel until tracking purpose, consent behavior, public wording, and data-sharing configuration are approved.

Change your choices

You may accept selected optional technologies, reject all optional technologies, or reopen your choices. The website remains usable when optional tracking is declined.
