Recruitment stages

What Happens After You Apply

  1. Application received. A reference is generated.
  2. Initial screening. Submitted qualifications are reviewed.
  3. Interview invitation. Shortlisted applicants receive scheduling instructions.
  4. Interview stages. The process may include initial and final interviews or an approved assessment.
  5. Orientation or next step. Qualified applicants receive clear instructions.
  6. Final status. Applicants may be progressed, placed in a talent pool, declined, withdrawn, or recorded as no-show.

Exact role classification, compensation, allowances, and contractual terms are provided only through authorized recruitment materials.
