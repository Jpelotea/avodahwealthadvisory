Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Avodah Wealth Advisory respects your privacy and handles website inquiries with care. This policy explains how information submitted through the website may be collected, used, stored, and shared for consultation and advisory coordination.

Information we collect

When you request a consultation, the website form may collect your full name, mobile number, email address, location, inquiry type, preferred contact method, and message.

How information is used

Information is used to respond to inquiries, prepare for consultation conversations, understand your requested service pathway, coordinate relevant next steps, and improve website inquiry handling.

Sharing with providers or partners

Avodah may discuss or coordinate with relevant accredited partners or providers only when appropriate for your inquiry and with reasonable consent or instruction. Final provider processes remain subject to each provider’s own requirements.

Sensitive information

Please do not submit account numbers, passwords, full government ID numbers, full medical records, bank login details, or other highly sensitive private information through the website form or public messaging channels.

Third-party services

This website may use Netlify for hosting and form handling, Google Analytics for website measurement, and external links to Facebook, Messenger, email, and phone services. These third-party platforms may process information under their own policies.

Your privacy requests

For privacy questions, access requests, correction requests, or deletion requests related to website inquiries, contact Avodah Wealth Advisory at avodahwealthadvisory@gmail.com.
