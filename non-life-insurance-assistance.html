Non-life insurance

Non-life insurance assistance for vehicles, property, business, cargo, and travel.

Avodah assists clients in preparing inquiries for comprehensive car insurance, CTPL, motorcycle insurance, fire and property insurance, SME insurance, marine cargo, contractor's all risk, electronic equipment, money insurance, surety bonds, and travel insurance.

Send an Insurance InquiryView Non-Life Services

Who this is for

  • Vehicle owners reviewing car, CTPL, motorcycle, or commercial vehicle insurance.
  • Homeowners and property owners reviewing fire, home, or property insurance.
  • Business owners preparing SME, marine cargo, contractor, equipment, money, or bond-related inquiries.
  • Travelers who need travel insurance preparation support.

Details to prepare

  • Type of property, vehicle, business asset, travel, or risk to be insured.
  • Ownership details, location, vehicle details, travel dates, or business background where relevant.
  • Existing policy details or renewal date if available.
  • Preferred contact method and urgency.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Waiting until renewal or travel date is too close.
  • Submitting unclear vehicle, property, or business details.
  • Assuming coverage, price, or approval before provider review.
  • Skipping questions about exclusions, limits, and claims process.

Coverage availability, eligibility, pricing, underwriting, policy terms, and final decisions remain subject to provider requirements.
